Yudhana Nanayakkara

Who Is Yudhana

Yudhana Nanayakkara is a writer & entrepreneur who pivoted from a path in medicine to forge a series of successful businesses and campaigns. He's been featured in Popular Mechanics and is an Amazon Kindle best-seller.

At the age of just 17, he co founded VEGAS a street clothing brand which is a celebration of youth culture & unapologetic self-expression of Sri Lankan youth. VEGAS is one of the fastest growing and one of the leading brands in Sri Lanka

He is currently serving the Royal College Red Cross Society as the 'Head of Information Technology' for the years 24/25, he also served the Royal College Science Association as the Quiz Team Organizer for the years 22/23
